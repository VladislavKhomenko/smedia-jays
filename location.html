Saskatoon

MOVING & STORAGE SERVICES

At Jay’s Transportation Group in Saskatoon we offer full commercial and household moving services and storage. Our warehouse provides secure short and long term storage as well as limited self storage.

As part of Jay’s Group our movers participate in a number of volunteer activities in support of charitable organizations including:

  • Kinsmen Telemiracle
  • Special Olympics, and
  • Multiple Sclerosis Society of Canada

Jay’s Saskatoon is one of the largest locations in Jay’s Group of Companies, with a staff ranging seasonally from about 20 to as many as 40 hardworking professional movers.

let's get a move on!

Moving prices shouldn't be wrapped in mystery. Jay's goes the extra mile to give you an accurate quote, coming to your home or business at no charge to walk through all of your requirements. Here's some of the ground you and your Jay's moving consultant will cover:

  • weight of your shipment
  • distance to your destination
  • appliances (moving our and in)
  • crating of large or fragile items
  • storage in transit
  • packing and unpacking
  • packing supplies
  • logistical challenges like elevators and stairs, or if shuttle service is required
  • cargo protection beyond the standard protection we include

how do we price your move?

Local moves are priced by the hour. Out-of-town moves are priced by weight and distance. After our free in-home visit, Jay's guarantees your long distance move estimate within 10%! Just another reason why choosing Jay's is the right move.
